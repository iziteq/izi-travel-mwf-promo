IZI Audio Guides

Create your own free audio guide for a museum or outdoor tour, with our Content Management System (CMS) and the mobile app OPAS. It’s absolutely free!

IZI CMS

You can quickly make, test and publish your audio guide using our simple yet powerful content management system. Need help? Browse our help documentation.

Mobile app OPAS

A mobile app for visitors’ iOS and Android devices. You can access hundreds of audiostories about museums, parks, zoos, and even whole cities around the world.

How it works

Record

Record your audio stories. Use your own computer. No professional equipment is required!

Publish

Add text and pictures using IZI CMS. Publish your audio guide. It will instantly appear on the app.

Announce

Announce the mobile audio guide to your visitors. They can download the free mobile app and content using Wi‑Fi or 3G.
